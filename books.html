The Hobbyist Curator

The Hobbyist Curator is Merrick’s way of showing her love for books and extraordinary writing with the world via Instagram or “Bookstagram.” Here she will post about books she’s currently reading, reviews, book hauls, bookish news, and as well as some of her own personal writing journey.

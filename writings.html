Welcome To Andromeda!

Welcome to Andromeda! is Merrick’s collegiate Honors’ thesis, which is a science-fiction, choose-your-own-adventure screenplay that features no human characters in its’ cast. Merrick spent 10 months planning, writing, and rewriting Welcome to Andromeda! while finishing her studies at college. While the school assignment part is long done, the full script is not however. Merrick plans to finish it and hopefully one day see it on the big screen, alongside her inspirations for the project.

Meet Merrick

After attending two different high schools, Merrick decided it was time for an even bigger change and graduated the summer after her junior year. Following the tradition no one thought of, Merrick then graduated college with her Bachelor’s in English three short years later but amid a global pandemic. After college, Merrick jumped headfirst into the insurance world but quickly found out it wasn’t really her style. A couple short-term, odd jobs here and there later, Merrick decided it was time for a big change. That’s where SheCodes came in - teaching women how to code and start their careers in technology. Merrick quickly found out she thoroughly enjoyed coding and front-end development and has been efficiently working through the whole SheCodes curriculum.
In her personal life, Merrick enjoys a little bit of each of her hobbies on a regular basis - writing screenplays, reading, sharing books on Instagram, creating pixel art, and playing cozy games. Merrick spends her days alongside her three pets - a bearded dragon named Zelda, a cat named Ocarina, and a guinea pig named Hyrule - and her loving family, Mom and Dad, two older sisters, two brother-in-laws, a niece, and four doggy nieces and nephews.
